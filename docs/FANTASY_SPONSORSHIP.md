# Last Season fantasy sponsorship plan

## Positioning

Last Season is an independent fantasy-football decision helper, not a competing game. Sponsors can support useful tools and editorial briefings but cannot influence player rankings, fixture ratings or recommendations.

## Initial inventory

1. Fantasy hub presenting partner — one restrained mark and one sentence.
2. Weekly Gameweek briefing — one labelled sponsor block after the editorial answer.
3. Fixture planner — “supported by” placement outside the controls and table.
4. Team-name lab — optional sponsor on the generator and share-card footer.
5. Mini-league kit — relevant prize partner, clearly identified.

## Sales rules

- No gambling partners during the current growth phase.
- No paid inclusion in player rankings.
- No sponsor scripts with independent tracking before consent review.
- No exclusivity longer than one season in the first agreement.
- Use fixed-fee tests before impression-based pricing.

## Evidence to share privately

- GA4 sessions and active users for the latest 30 days.
- Organic-search share.
- Top fantasy landing pages.
- Average engagement time after the current UX release.
- Name generate, copy, save and share completion rates.
- Newsletter interest count and geography only in aggregate.

## First outreach categories

- Licensed football merchandise and retro-shirt retailers.
- Mini-league trophies, engraving and personalised gifts.
- Football podcasts, magazines and fan communities.
- Broadband, streaming or productivity brands with a natural deadline/weekend fit.
- Non-gambling fantasy analysis and data services where the partnership does not duplicate Last Season’s core utility.
